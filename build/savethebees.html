Save the Bees

Hey kids, we here at Haunted Bees love bees, but as fun as spooky ghost bees are, we'd rather have real bees be alive! Bees are in a bit of a crisis right now due to climate change (ask your parents or teachers if they support climate action, and if they don't, ask them why they hate bees, sunshine, and happiness!), and we need to do all we can to save the bees! Below are some links to donation pages for various organizations dedicated to saving the bees, as well as some general educational resources.
