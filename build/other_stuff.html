Other Stuff
This is the home to Haunted Bees' smaller games, mostly made for game jams, mostly playable in your (computer) browser, and all free! Also, some other non-game projects, since we do those sometimes, too!
Food Portal

This is basically a food blog with extra steps. It's got an encyclopedia of spices, reviews of vegan food substitutes, and my attempts to cook food from everywhere in the world.

See the international food journey! Become a master of spices! Find the best non-dairy dairy, non-meat meat, and so on!
Olympus Mountain Creamery

Olympus Mountain Creamery is an anarchy simulator created for the 2022 1-Button Jam hosted by Feldo. Use the power of morse code to defeat fascism!

Play it now in your browser!
Morse Morse Revolution

Morse Morse Revolution is a game about making yogurt, made for the Solo Dev Game Jam #4, which had a theme of "Two Button Controls."

Play it now in your browser, or download it!
Bee Questions!

Bee Questions! is an online A&Q site - instead of asking questions and getting answers, you give the answer and then others must come up with questions for it! Join in and have some fun! Who knows, maybe you'll learn something!*

* You won't.

Play it now in your browser!
Sonic

Sonic is a database of corporate ownership and activity, for when you want to avoid buying from a big corporation that kills babies*, but they have so many subsidiaries that make it hard to figure out who's who. This site tells you who owns what, and lists some things those companies have done that you might want to be aware of.

* This example is not an exaggeration or joke. It's Nestle.

Start browsing now!
Zenn Halsey Basesol '93

Zenn Halsey Basesol '93 is a game we stole from the future with our Reach Into The Future And Grab Video Games From It Machine. Or it's a browser game made for the April 2020 Sports 2 Jam hosted by Jon Terp. It's like baseball in space with floaty hands and constellations.

Play it now in your browser!
DataBee

DataBee is a checklist, note-taking, and recipe managing app. I made it because the checklist app I was using before kept bugging me to register an account and sync my data with the cloud, and I had absolutely no intention of doing that, but there was no way to turn off the notifications. Thus, I made my own. Great if you want something slightly more advanced than whatever note-taking app is built into your phone, but not something super beefy.

Use it now in your browser!
Public Domains: Gacha Game

Public Domains: Gacha Game is a mobile RPG that is mostly just a parody of mobile gacha games; but instead of getting animé girls, you get public domain art. Made for April Fools 2019.

Play in now in your browser or download it for Android!
Funeral Procession in Space

Funeral Procession in Space is a morbid space adventure made for the March 2019 Pizza Jam. It's exactly what the name says it is.

Play it now in your browser!
Wheel of Slime

Wheel of Slime lets you compete with your friends for fantastic prizes which are also slime. Another #classic 'randomly generated people and text-to-speech can be funny' game. Made for the April 2018 Slime Jam.

Play it now in your browser (Firefox recommended)!
