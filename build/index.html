Welcome to Haunted Bees Productions, the only indie video game studio! We're hard at work on our next game, Uprooted: Meal Replacement Game, but we've got lots of other video computer games for you to check out in the meantime!

If you want to destroy your foes in a fast-paced competitive tile-stacking game, check out Blockara, available for purchase on Steam or itch.io!

Or if a more casual, portable, puzzle experience is what you crave, Crop Drop is guaranteed fresh, with several crop dropping and popping game modes to experience!

And, of course, if you're more into the "rooty tooty point-and-shooty" genre, look no further than Sonic Shock, a short but deadly romp through a devastated space colony that you can download and play for free from itch.io!
