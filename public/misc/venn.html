VennMa

You wanna make a quick venn diagram? I gotchu covered. No signups, no bullshit. Type text, get diagram. Change colors if you want to.

The first sections are for your sets. Title them, then enter values - line by line - that are unique to them. After that are the intersections; fill them in with the shared values - line by line.

Once the diagram looks how you want it, just click the Save button at the bottom (or right click and click the Save option if you're on a computer). It's yours, my friend. Now just pick what kind of diagram you want. More coming soon, maybe.

* 4-Value is not a true diagram as A+D and B+C are not shown. Work In Progress.
